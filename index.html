// why arcan
end-to-end encrypted every message is sealed on your device and opened on theirs — no one in between can read it. keys never touch a server, so there is nothing we can recover, decrypt, or hand over. your data is accessible to you, and only you. 01
open source & self-hostable every line is public — read it, audit it, or build it yourself. and if you'd rather run arcan on your own trusted server, one binary is all it takes: arcan ships to your server, not ours. github ↗ 02
local-first built on jazz ↗, arcan keeps everything on your device first. read, write, and organize while offline — the moment you're back online, every change syncs, still sealed. 03
